About Us

Kshetrajiva offers an integrated animal detection and deterrence system that combines sensors, water and chemical sprays, and camera technologies to prevent wild animals from entering farmlands.

Our Mission

“To develop and implement advanced virtual design and construction strategies that effectively manage animal interventions in agricultural lands, safeguarding farmers crops and ehancong environmental stewardship.”

Key Features:

  • Automatic detection of animal movement using sensors and cameras.
  • Solar-powered spray systems to deter animals using a mix of water and natural chemical repellents.
  • Environmentally friendly, scalable, and easy to operate.
